Generations Diamonds

Web Design

The Client

Generations Diamonds, a local secondhand jewelry buyer.

The Problem

Many jewelry buyers can feel a bit…pawn shop-py and Generations wanted to avoid that association at all cost. They are true experts and needed a website that would make customers feel comfortable coming to them for appraisal and resale.

The Solution

Their new site feels upscale and trustworthy, funneling customers to get in touch with Generations for an appraisal.

Technologies Used

WordPress, HTML, CSS
