Killer Shine

Print Design

The Project

Killer Shine, a southern fiction novel in the vein of Carl Hiaasen.

The Goal

The author wanted to pay homage to other southern fiction novels with a cover that leaned toward colorful illustration without losing the textured, material look she loved.

My Role

Cover and Layout Design, press preparation for Createspace
