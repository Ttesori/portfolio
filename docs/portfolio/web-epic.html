Epic Universe

Web Design

The Client

Epic Universe is a series of books published by author Lee Stephen. A sci-fi series for lovers of Halo and Star Wars, the author loves the epic feel of those all-time greats.

The Problem

Most websites for self-published authors look, well, a little DIY. Lee wanted his website to have a “big-screen blockbuster” kind of feel and draw in an audience that aren’t conventional readers.

The Solution

On Epic Universe’s new website, Lee’s custom artwork jumps off the page, his varied content resonates clearly and the whole thing certainly feels more “video game” than book!

Technologies Used

WordPress, HTML, CSS
