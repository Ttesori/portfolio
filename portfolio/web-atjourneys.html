At Journey's End

Web Design

The Client

At Journey’s End, a local bed and breakfast.

The Problem

Bed and breakfasts have a stuffy, traditional reputation — precisely the opposite of the modern, elegant vibe At Journey’s End wanted to portray.

The Solution

Their new website is clean and contemporary, putting the focus on the beautifully adorned bed and breakfast itself.

Technologies Used

Wordpress, HTML, CSS, Photography
